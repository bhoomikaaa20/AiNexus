AI Technologies

The engines driving the AI revolution.

Neural Networks

Modeled loosely after the human brain, artificial neural networks consist of thousands or even millions of simple processing nodes that are densely interconnected. They are excellent at recognizing patterns in data.

Deep Learning

A subset of machine learning based on artificial neural networks with multiple layers (deep neural networks). It is behind many of the recent massive breakthroughs in AI, from computer vision to speech recognition.
