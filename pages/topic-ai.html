What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions. The term may also be applied to any machine that exhibits traits associated with a human mind such as learning and problem-solving.

Key Characteristics

  • Learning: The ability to acquire knowledge and understand how to apply it.
  • Reasoning: Using rules to reach approximate or definite conclusions.
  • Self-Correction: Adjusting parameters and rules based on outcomes to improve future performance.

AI can be categorized into two broad types: Narrow AI (which is designed to perform a specific task, like facial recognition or web searches) and General AI (which has broad cognitive abilities that rival human intelligence, currently a theoretical concept).
